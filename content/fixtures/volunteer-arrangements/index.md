This page applies to all volunteers signing up to a WREC'kd fixture. By submitting a registration you confirm you have read and agree to the points below.

### Arrangements

Thank you for volunteering with WREC'kd. Volunteers are the backbone of our events and we are grateful for your time and energy.

This event takes place in and around remote New Zealand backcountry terrain. As a volunteer you may be stationed in exposed locations for extended periods. Please ensure you are appropriately dressed and equipped for the conditions on the day. The Race Director will be in touch with further details.

WREC'kd is committed to providing a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment for all participants, volunteers, and spectators. All participants are expected to conduct themselves with respect and integrity at all times, to look after themselves and others, and know their limits when in the backcountry.

### Declaration

By registering as a volunteer, you confirm that you have disclosed any medical conditions or medications relevant to participation, either on the registration form or in prior correspondence with the Race Director.

You have kept your emergency contact informed of your participation in this activity. You are aware of the risks associated with the environment in which this event takes place, and confirm you are aware of the risks as stated on the RAMS, and are volunteering of your own free will.
